A STORY OF UNLUCKY PERSON:

“Those who cannot change their minds cannot change anything” . These lines are always true, I won’t argue in this. I here to convey a short story of a unluckiest person who has a dramatic changes in himself in recent years!


A man enjoying his life as possible as he can around bunches of problem. slowly every problems around him start to interfere in his life. These changes made himself as a new man with a stone hearted. What the changes means:


Once a lovable person now as man with rugged soul..... Once he had a lot friends, now stand alone in a darkened forest with a blindfold..... Once a person who loved by someone now as a person who disturbing them..... Once a person who can adopt himself at any surroundings now as a man who can’t able to adopt himself in a surrounding after survive there for a period of nearly 13 months.....


Once a person who can attract others with his speech and attitude now as a person with attitude of hiding himself behind something else..... Once a person who approach different type people and capable of making a bond with them, now as a person who find difficulty in finding a person who can sink with his thoughts..... Once a person who holed belief of lot of people now as a person who can’t hold belief on himself....Once a man who bored of replying in chats now as a man only receiving messages as unreplyable …..

Once a person who have interest to learn new things, now as a person who can’t even focus in his own subjects..... Once a person with lot of goals for his life, now as a person who loose his future and doing such things in which he had no interests.....Once a person who was a model to be for others, now as a person who was a model not to be.....

Once a person who had a ability to won conversion with others, now as a person who can’t able win a video games.....Once a person who making memories, now as a man who live with his past memories.....


Once a person who could able to be a understandable person, now as a person who has lot of misunderstandings with others accidently..... Once a person who throw away the sour happenings, now as a person who can’t even forget a person in his dreams..... Once a person who lived a life as a dream for others, now as a person who can’t even came out from his imaginary life ..... A person who want to change to a former him, but unable to find a way to achieve it.....
